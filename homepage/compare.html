Dual-Language Challenge

The Japanese and Chinese writing systems share a lot in common. If you can read one well, chances are that you can suss out some meaning in the other.
In this game, you can choose which language you know better— Japanese or Chinese—and then see how well you can read the other language.
When you press Go!, after a few moments, you'll see a title from a Wikipedia article in one language, and then multiple choice beneath it. One of those multiple choices matches, the others are random.
Can you guess the right one?
